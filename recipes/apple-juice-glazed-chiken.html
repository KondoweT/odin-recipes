Apple Juice-Glazed Chicken

Apple Juice-Glazed Chicken Recipe

This chicken is smothered in a delicious apple juice reduction. It's quick, simple, and a definite keeper. Serve the chicken and sauce over rice for an easy meal.

This is a quick meal to cook, one would only need 5 minutes to prepare, 20 minutes to cook with no addional. The total time it takes to make 4 serving assuming one follows the recipe below is 25 minutes.

Ingredients

  • cooking spray
  • 1 (16 ounce) package chicken tenders
  • 1 teaspoon Greek seasoning, or to taste
  • 3/4 cup apple juice
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice

Steps

  1. Spray a large nonstick skillet with cooking spray; heat over medium-high heat.
  2. Season chicken tenders with Greek seasoning and add to hot skillet. Cook chicken until no longer pink in the center and the juices run clear, about 4 minutes per side. Remove chicken to a plate and cover to keep warm.
  3. Pour apple juice and lemon juice into the hot skillet and bring to a boil while scraping the browned pieces off the bottom of the skillet with a wooden spoon. Cook sauce, stirring occasionally, until apple juice reduces and sauce thickens, about 10 minutes. Add chicken to skillet and stir until evenly coated with sauce.
