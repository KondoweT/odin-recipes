Greek Chicken Skewers Recipe

Bright, fresh and flavorful Greek Chicken Kebabs! Chicken breasts are perfectly portioned into bite size pieces, soaked in a delicious marinade then threaded onto skewer sticks with lots of fresh vegetables and grilled (or baked).

The preperation should take about 20 minutes and 20 minutes to cook. However, one can make this meal more interesting by adding whatever they would like to take with grilled chicken, this would roughly add about 2 hours to the cook time bring the total cook time to 2 hours 40 minutes. The total Servings for this recipe is 4.

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup lemon juice
  • 1/4 cup wok oil
  • 1/8 cup red wine
  • 1 tablespoon onion flakes
  • 1 tablespoon minced garlic
  • 1 lemon, zested
  • 1 teaspoon Greek seasoning
  • 1 teaspoon poultry seasoning
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 3 skinless, boneless chicken breast, or as needed, cut into 1-inch pieces
  • 8 bamboo skewers, or as needed, soaked in water for 30 minutes

Steps

  1. Whisk lemon juice, oil, vinegar, onion flakes, garlic, lemon zest, Greek seasoning, poultry seasoning, oregano, pepper, and thyme together in a bowl and pour into a resealable plastic bag.
  2. Add chicken, coat with the marinade, squeeze out excess air, and seal the bag. Marinate in the refrigerator for at least 2 hours.
  3. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  4. Remove chicken from the marinade and shake off excess; thread onto skewers. Discard the remaining marinade. Place the skewers on a baking sheet.
  5. Roast in the preheated oven until golden brown, about 20 minutes.
