Greek Chicken Skewers Recipe

Bright, fresh and flavorful Greek Chicken Kebabs! Chicken breasts are perfectly portioned into bite size pieces, soaked in a delicious marinade then threaded onto skewer sticks with lots of fresh vegetables and grilled (or baked).

The preperation should take about 20minutes and 20minutes to cook. However, one can make this meal more interesting by adding whatever they would like to take with grilled chicken, this would roughly add about 2 hours to the cook time bring the total cook time to 2 hours 40 minutes. The total Servings for this recipe is 4.

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup lemon juice
  • 1/4 cup wok oil
  • 1/8 cup red wine
  • 1 tablespoon onion flakes
  • 1 tablespoon minced garlic
